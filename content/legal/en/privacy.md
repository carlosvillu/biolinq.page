# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you sign up for BioLinq using Google OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We use this information to create and manage your account.

### 1.2 Usage Data

We collect analytics data about how you use BioLinq, including page views, link clicks, device information, browser type, and IP address. This helps us understand how our service is being used and how we can improve it.

### 1.3 Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, storing your preferences, and analytics. Essential cookies are required for the website to function properly. Analytics cookies help us understand user behavior and improve our service.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve BioLinq
- Create and manage your account
- Process payments for premium features via Stripe
- Send you important updates about your account, service changes, or security issues
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
- Respond to your support requests

## 3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Stripe**: For processing payments when you purchase premium features
- **Google**: For authentication via Google OAuth
- **Google Analytics**: For understanding how users interact with our service
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through your account settings. After deletion, we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt out of non-essential analytics cookies

To exercise these rights, please contact us or use the account settings in your dashboard.

## 6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

BioLinq is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date below.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@biolinq.page.
